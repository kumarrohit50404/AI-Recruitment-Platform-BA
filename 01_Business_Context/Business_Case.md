## 1. Executive Summary

This business case justifies a **$1.85M investment** in HireFlow AI, an enterprise recruitment platform. Analysis demonstrates a **340% ROI over 3 years**, **14-month payback period**, and **$6.2M cumulative net benefit** for a 2,000-employee organization. The platform addresses $3.8M in annual hiring inefficiency costs while creating a new SaaS revenue stream projected at **$2.4M ARR by Year 2**.

**Recommendation:** Proceed with full funding and Phase 1 MVP delivery by Q3 2026.

---

## 2. Current State Analysis

### 2.1 Hiring Process Today (AS-IS)

```
Job Requisition → Manual Posting → Resume Email Flood → Manual Screening (23 hrs/wk)
    → Phone Screen → Schedule Interviews (6+ emails) → Panel Interviews
    → Subjective Evaluation → Offer Approval (3-5 days) → Onboarding Handoff
```

### 2.2 Current State Metrics

| Process Step | Avg. Time | Cost | Pain Points |
|--------------|-----------|------|-------------|
| Resume collection | Ongoing | $0 | Multiple channels, duplicates |
| Resume screening | 23 hrs/recruiter/week | $28,750/yr per recruiter | 75% unqualified, fatigue |
| Interview scheduling | 4.5 hrs/requisition | $5,625/yr per req | Calendar conflicts, no-shows |
| Interview execution | 3.2 hrs/candidate | $8,000/yr per req | Inconsistent questions |
| Evaluation & scoring | 2.1 hrs/candidate | $5,250/yr per req | Bias, no standardization |
| Offer preparation | 1.5 days | $3,750/offer | Manual templates, delays |
| Reporting | 8 hrs/month (HR) | $12,000/yr | Excel-based, stale data |

### 2.3 Technology Landscape

| System | Purpose | Limitation |
|--------|---------|------------|
| Legacy ATS (Taleo) | Application tracking | No AI, poor UX, 2018 version |
| Email/Outlook | Communication | No tracking, lost threads |
| Excel | Reporting | Manual, error-prone |
| Zoom | Video interviews | No integration, no scoring |
| SharePoint | Document storage | No parsing, version chaos |

### 2.4 Organizational Impact

- **5 recruiters** handling **180 requisitions/year** = 36 reqs/recruiter (industry avg: 45)
- **34% recruiter annual turnover** due to burnout from repetitive tasks
- **22% offer decline rate** attributed to slow process (candidates accept competing offers)
- **3 compliance incidents** in past 18 months related to inconsistent evaluation documentation
