# Business Case — HireFlow AI
## AI-Powered Recruitment Platform (Portfolio Case Study)

**Author:** Rohit Kumar | Business Analyst | June 2026

---

## 1. Business Problem

Mid-size organizations struggle with slow, manual hiring. Recruiters spend significant time screening resumes. HR leaders lack real-time visibility into the hiring pipeline. Interview scheduling and candidate evaluation are inconsistent across teams.

**Current pain points:**

| Area | Challenge |
|------|-----------|
| Screening | Recruiters spend ~23 hours per week reading resumes manually |
| Scheduling | Multiple email exchanges to book interviews; calendar conflicts |
| Evaluation | Subjective feedback; no standard scorecard |
| Visibility | HR relies on spreadsheets; pipeline status is outdated |
| Compliance | Difficulty explaining AI-assisted screening decisions |

---

## 2. Proposed Solution

**HireFlow AI** is a concept for an AI-assisted recruitment platform that would:

- Rank candidates against job requirements so recruiters review top matches first
- Provide explainable AI scores with human override and audit trail
- Support structured interview scheduling and digital evaluation scorecards
- Give HR and leadership a hiring funnel dashboard with core KPIs

*This portfolio documents the business analysis for this solution — it is a case study, not a deployed product.*

---

## 3. Expected Benefits

| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Faster screening | Recruiters focus on ranked shortlists instead of reading every resume |
| Better visibility | Executive dashboard shows funnel conversion, time-to-hire, and recruiter performance |
| Consistent evaluation | Digital scorecards and structured feedback |
| Compliance support | Explainable AI rationale and audit logging for screening decisions |
| Improved candidate experience | Self-scheduling and clearer application status |
